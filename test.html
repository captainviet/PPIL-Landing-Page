About the Event

PayPal is ushering in the Future of Money with the launch of its brand new PayPal Innovation Lab in Singapore. As a leader in digital payments, PayPal is bringing together its expertise and resources by fostering innovation and building capability that helps take Singapore to the next frontier in digital commerce and payments. This is a first-of-its-kind collaboration between PayPal and the Singapore FinTech ecosystem including government agencies, industry associations, consumers and universities. Through the PayPal Innovation Lab, PayPal endeavors to transform the community it is part of â€“ by enabling researchers, students, start-ups and small businesses to embrace FinTech innovation in topics close to their hearts, and contribute to shaping the Future of Money. This year to kick-start this initiative, PayPal is calling for SMEs in the F&B industry to step forward to share their challenges relating to payments.
